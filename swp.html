How SWP Values Are Calculated? ▼

Monthly SWP Calculation

Every month the balance grows with returns and then withdrawal is deducted:

Balance_new = (Balance_old × (1 + r)) − W

Where:
 • r = Monthly return (Annual Return% ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
 • W = Monthly Withdrawal

Simulation Loop

Repeat monthly until:
 • Balance becomes 0, OR
 • 200 years (2400 months) completed

Corpus Lasts (Without SWP)

This shows how long your corpus lasts WITHOUT any return:

Months_without_SWP = Corpus ÷ Withdrawal
Years_without_SWP  = Months_without_SWP ÷ 12
